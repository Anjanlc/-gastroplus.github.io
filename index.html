What is Piles ?

Piles, also known as hemorrhoids, are swollen veins in the lower rectum and anus. They can cause discomfort, itching, and bleeding, especially during bowel movements. Piles can be internal or external, depending on their location. Internal piles occur inside the rectum and are usually not visible during an external examination. External piles form small lumps on the outside edge of the anus. Piles can be caused by increased pressure in the lower rectum due to chronic constipation, chronic diarrhea, lifting heavy weights, or straining when passing a stool. Treatment options for piles include over-the-counter creams and ointments, lifestyle changes, and surgical procedures.

If you are experiencing symptoms of piles, it is best to consult a medical professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.

what is Laser Proctology Surgery?

Laser proctology is a minimally invasive surgical treatment of diseases of the colon, rectum, and anus using a laser. Common conditions treated with laser proctology include hemorrhoids, fissures, fistula, pilonidal sinus, and polyps.

Laser ablation uses a high-energy laser to accurately treat affected areas of tissues without pain or bleeding. The technique is increasingly being used to treat piles in both men and women. The laser is the latest modality for fissures, fistulas, and piles. It is a daycare procedure that offers several advantages over traditional surgery.
